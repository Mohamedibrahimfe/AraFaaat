## Selected Design: الحداثة الإسلامية المتناغمة (Islamic Modern Harmony)

I have chosen **Islamic Modern Harmony** as the design philosophy for the Arafah Day website. This approach balances contemporary minimalism with Islamic spiritual aesthetics, creating a serene and meditative experience that honors the sacred nature of the day while remaining accessible and modern.

### Design Decision Rationale

This design philosophy was selected because it:
- Creates a spiritually appropriate atmosphere without being overly ornate
- Provides excellent readability for Arabic text with elegant typography
- Uses geometric patterns subtly to reinforce Islamic identity
- Encourages meditative interaction through thoughtful animations
- Scales well across different screen sizes while maintaining elegance

### Key Design Elements to Implement

1. **Color Palette:** Deep Islamic Green + Warm Gold + Soft Cream
2. **Typography:** Arabic Droid Sans for primary content + Cormorant Garamond for English
3. **Layout:** Asymmetric vertical flow with floating cards and breathing space
4. **Patterns:** Subtle Girih geometric borders and Islamic decorative elements
5. **Animations:** Gentle, meditative transitions that encourage reflection
6. **Interactions:** Haptic-inspired feedback on counter clicks with pulse effects
